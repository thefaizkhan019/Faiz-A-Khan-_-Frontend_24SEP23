The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound, similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The Beagle was developed primarily for hunting here, known as beagling. Processing a great sence of smell and superior tracking instincts, the beagle is the primary breed used as a detection dog for prohibited ariculture imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. The beagle is intelligent and is a popular pet due to its size, good temper, and a lack of inherited health problems.
